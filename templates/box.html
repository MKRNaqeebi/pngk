The RPbox enabling data-driven decisions in places with unreliable infrastructure.

The RPbox is a portable server which primary function is to control and collect data from incoming and outgoing calls and text messages. This product is designed for organizations working in low and lower-middle income economies and can maintain functionality during intermittent periods of no power or internet. For security, all data can be stored locally.

The RPbox, in combination with PNGK’s data science experts is your ‘one stop shop’ for all of your information gathering and processing needs.
